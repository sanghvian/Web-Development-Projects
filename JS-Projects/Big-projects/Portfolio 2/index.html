About Me

When you compete with yourself, your only limit is you.

Hi !

I'm a 19 year old inquisitive Indian kid. I'm fascinated by technology and am always willing to know what drives it. We are currently witnessing the Renaissance of technology and I am super pumped to know about it as much as I can.


Childhood

I had a pretty amazing childhood growing up in Mumbai, India. Fortunately, the smartphone era had not taken over so I spent most evenings playing for hours together with my friends. We would play, argue, fight, talk, laugh, share and create memories the entire time. I spent my vacations travelling all over India or exploring different sports or sci-tech classes.


Growing up and Engineering

As I grew up, I became more and more investigative on a medley of topics such as technology, politics, history, religion, life and philosophy. Why do we do this ?How does this work ? Can we make it better ? How can we make it better ?

Thus, engineering came naturally to me. Not the boring theoretical parts as much as the practical application. The art of creating things. Fixing things. Making them better.
Some of my fondest memories include hours spent fixing a broken tape recorder or watching a carpenter build furniture, an electrician repair air conditioner, watching the cycle shop employee repair my cycle.

The need for answers to my questions and fixes to my problems propelled me towards the art of engineering. The art of invention itself.It's an amazing feeling, adrenaline coursing through your veins as you try the 100th iteration on a CAD software, hoping that this model will render successfully.


I started my programming journey when I wasn't even old enough to understand what it was. For me, the spark began with game development in Java. I honestly didn't even know it was called programming. For me, it was just a bunch of words that when typed together, gave back a pretty cool game. I was fascinated by this world since then.

That was 7 years ago. Yet I still view programming as this awesome tool that empowers you to create beautiful things and help society. Currently, I am an established web developer with skills encompassing everything from the basics of HTML to CSS Frameworks like Materialize and Bootstrap and even JavaScript and its libraries and popular frameworks.

Last I checked,

I'm a 2nd year undergraduate pursuing a BTech Degree in Civil Engineering. I explore vehicle modelling and programming for fun. That's about it, till the next update !
